Welcome to Denise's Child Care Center

Denise' Child Care Center based in Bronx, provides innovative and affordable concept in childcare and preschool education.

Our child care center offers excellent facilities suitable for children ages 5 weeks to school aged. Our program is designed to prepare the children for their scholastic years ahead. We also offer after school and planned activities to keep the children busy with age appropriate groups and completing their school homeworks.
